Keep an eye on your score and data

While you are enrolled in myCredit Assist you will receive each of your reports
to see what's changed and what it means to your FICO score. A credit advisor
will show you how your credit actions are affecting your FICO score and your
borrowing power.  During this process you will be able to see items that have
been investigated be deleted or updated. You will learn the criteria it takes to
improve your FICO score and ultimately maintaining a better credit history.
