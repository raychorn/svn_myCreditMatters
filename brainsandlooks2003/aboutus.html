About Our Business
Once you are a member, you will receive all 3 credit reports. We will begin by helping you read
and understand the various elements in your credit reports and  detail how your credit history
impacts your FICO scoring AND what you can do to improve and protect the information found
in your credit file.

Many of our clients prior to joining our program had never seen the information in their credit file
much less understood what categories they were scored in and the importance each plays in
their total score. It is our vision to ensure that as many people as possible see their credit
information and learn how to manage and protect their credit through life's major events.

We have fifteen plus years invested in credit information through professional relationships and
personally in the school of hard knocks. Our technical support is knowledgeable in the Fair
Credit Reporting Act and the Fair Debt Collecting Regulations and continually researches and
keeps abreast of new credit information as it relates to your credit file. We have the time and the
manpower to provide you with the individual attention that you need in order to achieve as high
of a score as possible.

What makes us unique is we strive to keep our costs low so we can pass that
SAVINGS along to you AND we allow you to earn back the hard-earned $$ you
spend by becoming a Gold Member in our Referral Program BUT don't take our
word for it, look to see what our clients say about us!
